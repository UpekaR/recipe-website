Description

A smooth, creamy baked cheesecake with a buttery crust and balanced sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 200g cream cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  2. Mix cream cheese and sugar until smooth.

  5. Bake for 45 minutes.
  6. Cool completely before serving.
