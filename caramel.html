Description

A silky, soft caramel pudding made with a classic Sri Lankan method.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 tbsp sugar (for pudding)

Instructions

  1. Melt sugar to make caramel and pour into a mold.
  2. Beat eggs and sugar together.
  3. Add warm milk and vanilla.
  4. Pour carefully over caramel.
  5. Steam for 20 minutes.
  6. Chill and serve cold.
