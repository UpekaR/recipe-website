A luxurious, coconut-milk-based tiger shrimp pasta with rich flavors and a silky sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200g tiger shrimp
  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp chili flakes
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer.
  5. Add orange pepper, soya sauce, sweet chili and mix in cooked pasta.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
